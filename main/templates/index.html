Any food, anywhere.

Get meals delivered from nearby restaurants for cheap, without the decision fatigue.

Hungry? Get the app. Are you a restuarant?

How it works (users)

  1. You place your order, easily choosing what features you'd like your meal to have.
  2. All of the nearby restaurants see your order, and bid on who can fulfill your order for the cheapest.
  3. You can then browse through the offers and choose which meal you'd like to accept.
  4. The meal is delivered right to your door.

How it works (restaurants)

  1. Your restaurant is in off-peak hours, and could use some more orders to fill. The stove is hot so why not sell more food!
  2. Sign up and have your menu uploaded to our site
  3. Browse through all the different meals people are looking for, matched directly to the meals your restaurant serves.
  4. Cook the meal, and have it picked up and delivered to the customer for you, via Uber.
