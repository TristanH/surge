How it works

  • You place your order, easily choosing what features you'd like your meal to have.
  • All of the nearby restaurants see your order, and bid on who can fulfill your order for the cheapest.
  • You can then browse through the offers and choose which meal you'd like to accept.
  • The meal is delivered right to your door.
  • You can scale your app, manage it, and deploy over 150 add-on services, from the Dashboard or CLI.
  • Check out the Getting Started guide to learn more!
